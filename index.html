About Mikiale

I started photographing in 2018 and have been chasing honest moments ever since.

I worked professionally as a photographer in Nairobi before moving to Italy, where I now live and shoot in Florence. My background is documentary photography — telling stories through real, unguarded images — and I bring that same eye to every portrait session.

That means no stiff, forced poses. I'll guide you, find the best light, and capture you as you actually are, with Florence as your backdrop.
